Brightness model

Magnitude is a deliberately rough diffuse-area proxy. It does not model attitude, BRDF, solar-array glints, asteroid phase laws, or sky brightness, so it is a guide for “worth trying,” not a prediction.

Synced geometry

Drag to orbit. Scroll, trackpad-scroll, or pinch to reveal more or less of the trajectory while Earth stays the same size; double-click resets the view and range. The translucent sheet is the ecliptic plane.

Best-spot scan

Global search for the single brightest rough-magnitude observing geometry. It first scans a coarse grid, then refines locally around the optimum. No terrain/clouds/airspace.
